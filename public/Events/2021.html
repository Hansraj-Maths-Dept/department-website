Pi-logue Competition '21

To celebrate the practical significance of Pi, the department celebrated Pi Day by organizing a "Pi-Logue" competition in which there were two categories, first, to recite the maximum number of digits of Pi after decimal, and, second, to recite the first 50 digits of Pi after the decimal in minimum time. Some spectacular records were set, the winner of the first category recited 400 digits after the decimal and the winner of second category recited 50 digits after decimal in 6.71 seconds.

Freshers '21

With the onset of the new academic session, the department organized a 'Meet and Greet' session to extend a hearty welcome to the freshers. The session was conducted on an online platform, keeping in mind the restrictions and precautionary measures posed by COVID-19. It was enthralling to see the cheerful faces and creative minds, all geared up for college. The event commenced with a short welcome message delivered by the department heads and a video showcasing life at Hansraj, followed by a brief introduction of teachers and department heads. Both, students and teachers participated with great zest and zeal in all the activities. The event wrapped up with an enjoyable interaction between the juniors and seniors to provide a taste of college life to the freshers.

Farewell '21

To bid goodbyes to the batch 2021, the department organized an online farewell, which consisted of poetry, music, games and what not. Everyone was overwhelmed, even the teachers, while addressing the outgoing batch. This is how the batch of 2021 officially graduated!

Teachers' Day '21

“Better than a thousand days of diligent study is one day with a great teacher.” Our department is blessed to have many such teachers with us. So, to thank these gems, the students organized an online Teachers’ Day program. The event began with a warm welcome of the teachers after which they shared their experiences with the students. The event was planned and executed successfully with amazing dance performances, beautiful songs, and poems by the students, and a video message for the teachers. Everyone participated with great enthusiasm and the teachers enjoyed the event to their fullest and appreciated students for their efforts.
