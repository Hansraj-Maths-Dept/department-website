The Annual Journal

Mathematics department of Hansraj college has always tried to expand its boundaries and bring forward new works to keep the legacy of the department go on forever. In accordance to this the Department of Mathematics released its first edition of AANKALAN: The Annual Journal of Department of Mathematics, Hansraj College. The publication contains expository as well as research articles, mathematical puzzles, facts and interviews with distinguished personalities in the field of mathematics. The articles themselves are very informative and also give a glimpse of the applications of mathematics in the fields and activities in which anyone could barely think that mathematics could play a crucial role. The journal provides you very handy information regarding future in mathematics.
