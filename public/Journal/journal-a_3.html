The Department Journal (2022)

After witnessing an overwhelming response for the previous two editions, we are ecstatic to announce the third edition of Aankalan, the Annual Journal of the Department of Mathematics, Hansraj College. Aankalan was started in 2019 with the objective of sparking research interest in students and providing them the encouragement and platform for the same.

This edition, the longest and most comprehensive one yet, includes papers exploring pure mathematics through set theory, group theory and the concepts of infinities, as well as the applications of mathematics to map projection, the calculus of variations, decryption and cryptography. It also features introductions to some intriguing domains like The Grandi’s Series, Conway’s Game of Life and a particular IMO Problem. We’re extremely honoured to also feature an interview with Dr. Neena Gupta, Ramanujan Prize Awardee, 2021.

We had a lifetime worth of experience putting this together and could not have done it without the support of our teachers, the council and of course, the students of the department themselves. We’re extremely grateful for the support and guarantee it’s plenty of food for thought so what’s the wait for? We look forward to hearing your thoughts on this edition. Let us know at aankalanhansraj@gmail.com
